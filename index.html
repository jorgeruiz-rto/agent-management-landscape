How to read the territory. The orange center is the AI gateway zone: every vendor inside it ships an AI gateway, per Gartner's May 2026 vendor list, and the zone deliberately overlaps the API incumbents, the AI-natives, the hyperscalers, and the governance platforms, because that is where the $250M-to-$1B gateway market is being contested. Gravitee sits at its center as the only neutral, enterprise-grade plane spanning API, event, model, MCP, and agent traffic. IBM occupies a triple point: API incumbent, AIGP Leader, and gateway provider. Airia is the governance vendor that crossed into the zone with an MCP gateway. Google and Microsoft fight from the hyperscaler front with frontier models of their own, and the model vendors sit just outside the zone, building agent stacks that govern only themselves. The data and context economy on the western flank (Salesforce, Databricks, Snowflake, Confluent) owns the context agents consume, not the traffic between context, models, and agents. That traffic is the seam Gravitee claims.
